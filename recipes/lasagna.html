Lasagna

A lovely beef lasagna that is both hearty and easy to make

This is a simple take on the classic italian dish that stays true to the flavours of the original,

Ingredients:

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean minced beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (or to taste)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 ounces of cottage cheese
  • ½ cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups tomato ∧ basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna sheets and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, place minced beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until beef is crumbly and evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs together in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  5. Lay 4 lasagna sheets side by side on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking pan; top with a layer of prepared tomato-basil sauce, a layer of minced beef mixture, and a layer of cottage cheese mixture. Repeat layers twice more, ending with a layer of sauce; sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the lasagna is bubbling and the cheese has melted, about 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake until cheese has begun to brown, about 10 more minutes. Allow to stand at least 10 minutes before serving.
